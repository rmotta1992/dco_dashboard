Summary Analysis

Tesla’s stock had consistently grown since 2009. Volume of trading has drastically gone up especially in 2013 and 2020, rising almost 700% and 200% respectively. Coinciding with this growth in trade, the opening price of the stock has also gone up significantly, especially in 2020. In the beginning the stock was not extremely volatile on a daily basis but after the larger amount of trading it has experienced large daily swings in price can be observed. Large changes in both positive and negative directions can be seen in the months of August and September 2018.
